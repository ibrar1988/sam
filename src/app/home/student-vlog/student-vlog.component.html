Student's Vlog

Hi, my name is Alec Jones, a creator of Christopher Bot which is an awesome tool that keeps track of students' homework and helps them prepare for every class. Pretty cool, right? Other than that, I'm just a 14-year-old high school student from Victoria, BC Canada.

As a 10th grader, having bad hair days or recurring nightmares about showing up to an exam I didn’t study for are just some of the high school problems that I face every day. But the biggest challenge of all is that I have to start thinking about getting to college! I'm beginning to ask myself questions like: ‘which college should I choose?’, ‘what are my chances of getting into college?’, ‘what should I do to get into the college of my dreams?’, ‘how will I be able to afford to pay for college?’ If you are in high school too, I'm sure you can relate to these concerns.

So, I thought I'd document my day to day life as a high school student and share my journey to college with you guys. Here are some of my vlogs. Thanks for watching.
