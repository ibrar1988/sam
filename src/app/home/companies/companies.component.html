For Companies

In the 21st century, employers face three obstacles between them and retaining talented employees:

  • Lifetime employment is a thing of the past. Employees are less loyal to their employers, switching jobs the moment better opportunities arise.

  • More so than any other time in history, employee's desire for work-life balance influences their career choices.

  • Employees care deeply about their children's education, which can negatively affect their job-focus.

Approximately 70% of employees who have high school-aged college-bound children are very or extremely concerned about college-related challenges and more than 50% recognize that they should be more involved in getting their child ready for college.
