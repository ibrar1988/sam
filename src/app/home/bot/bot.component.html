Christopher Bot

A personal homework assistant

Christopher Bot is a Facebook Messenger based bot that keeps track of student’s homework and helps them make sure that they are prepared for every class. It uses Facebook Messenger platform and a conversational language that fit into users’ lifestyle.

How it works

Created for Students by a Student

Alec Jones, a 15-year- old grade 10 student from Victoria, British Columbia, developed Christopher Bot to provide teenager like himself, with homework assistance. He is a great example of Generation Z entrepreneurial spirit.

MyKlovr and Christopher Bot partnership

MyKlovr acquired Christopher Bot in April 2017. It is part of the company social responsibility program “Built by Students, for Students” Tech Incubator, helping student endeavors to capitalize on their ingenious ideas surrounding education and development.
