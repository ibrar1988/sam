TUTOR / MENTOR / COACH

Are you a Student? Are you a Teacher? Are you both? Want to make incremental revenue by tutoring on the hottest new student development platform which just launch?

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • The tutor has to help the child or children in whatever subject they are having a difficulty with
  • They have to devise new methods of teaching if the child is having trouble learning through the conventional method
  • They can design workshops for the children so that they can learn better in different atmosphere than school
  • The tutor has to maintain a report about each child they work with so they can see the progress of the child
  • They have to be in contact with the teacher and the parents of the child, and they have to share information about the child
  • They have to trained to spot learning disorders and report them to the parents and the teacher
  • The tutors should have excellent communication skills, so they can interact with the childMost importantly, the tutors have to be well-versed in the curriculum they will be helping the students with
  • Though not mandatory, but it is an advantage if the tutor has some knowledge about child psychology
  • They have to ensure that the students improve their grades as this is their primary task

Education:
They have to have a bachelor's degree in education or a specialization in the subject they will be teachingAdditional experience in working with children

Job Type: Contract

Required education: High school or equivalent
